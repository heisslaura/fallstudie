FASTQ Format
Phred Quality Score

Phred quality score numerically expresses the accuracy of each nucleotide. Higher Q number signifies higher accuracy.
For example, if Phred assigns a quality score of 30 to a base, the chances of having base call error are 1 in 1000.
Phred Quality Score Q is calculated with -10log10(P), where p is probability of erroneous base call.
